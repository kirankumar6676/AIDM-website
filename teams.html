Organizers

Prof. Gunasekaran Raja

Gunasekaran Raja is working as a Professor in the Department of Computer Technology at Anna University, Chennai, where he is the Principal Investigator of the Next Generation Networks Lab (NGNLab). He is also the Director of the Internal Quality Assurance Cell of Anna University, Chennai. He was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the University of California, Davis, USA. He received a Ph.D. from the Faculty of Information and Communication Engineering, Anna University, Chennai, India, in 2010. His research interests include the Internet of Vehicles, UAV Communications, 5G/6G Networks, Blockchain, Cyber Security, Computer Vision, and Medical IoT. He was a recipient of the Young Engineer Award from the Institution of Engineers India in 2009, the FastTrack grant for Young Scientist from the Department of Science and Technology in 2011, the Professional Achievement Award in 2017 from IEEE Madras Section, and Visvesvaraya Young Faculty Research Fellowship from the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India, for the years 2019 and 2020, IEEE Publication Award for 2021, from IEEE Madras Section. He was recognized as one of the world’s top 2% of scientists in information and communication technology for 2022 by Stanford University, USA. He is a senior member of IEEE and ACM and a lifetime member of ISTE and CSI. Read More

Prof. Mohsen Guizani

Mohsen Guizani (Fellow, IEEE) received the B.S. (Hons.), M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in electrical and computer engineering from Syracuse University, Syracuse, NY, USA, in 1985, 1987, and 1990, respectively. He is currently a Professor in machine learning and an Associate Provost with the Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Previously, he worked in different institutions in the USA. He is the author of 11 books, over 1000 publications, and several U.S. patents. His research interests include applied machine learning and artificial intelligence, smart cities, the Internet of Things (IoT), intelligent autonomous systems, and cybersecurity. He was listed as a Clarivate Analytics Highly Cited Researcher in Computer Science in 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022. He has won several research awards, including the “2015 IEEE Communications Society Best Survey Paper Award,” the Best ComSoc Journal Paper Award in 2021, and five best paper awards from ICC and GLOBECOM Conferences. He was a recipient of the 2017 IEEE Communications Society Wireless Technical Committee (WTC) Recognition Award, the 2018 AdHoc Technical Committee Recognition Award, and the 2019 IEEE Communications and Information Security Technical Recognition (CISTC) Award. He served as the Editor-in-Chief for IEEE Network and is currently on the editorial boards of many IEEE Transactions and magazines. Read More

Dr. Kapal Dev

Kapal Dev is currently serving as an Assistant Lecturer at Munster Technological University (MTU), Ireland. He was awarded the PhD degree by Politecnico di Milano, Italy, in 2019 under the prestigious fellowship of Erasmus Mundus. He was recently awarded as IEEE ComSoc EMEA Outstanding Young Researcher 2022 for promising research activities for the benefit of the Society. He received The Tom Brazil Excellence in Research Award 2023 from SFI Funded CONNECT Research centre. He is serving as Associate Editor in IEEE Consumer Electronics Magazine, NATURE, Scientific Reports, Springer Wireless Networks, IET Quantum Communication, IET Networks, Editor in Elsevier Physical Communication, and Technical Committee Member in Elsevier COMCOM. He performed duties as Guest Editor (GE) in several Q1 journals, IEEE Network, IEEE TII, IEEE TNSE, and IEEE TGCN. He served(ing) as Lead workshop chair in one of ACM Mobicom 2022, IEEE Globecom 2023, 2022, IEEE Blockchain 2022, IEEE ICDCS 2022, IEEE CCNC 2021, IEEE Globecom 2021, IEEE PIMRC 2021 and ACM MobiCom 2021 workshops. He is a TPC member of IEEE Smart Cities 2022, IEEE BlackSeaCom 2022, IEEE Globecom 2022, IEEE ICC 2021, IEEE ICBC 2021, and IEEE VTC 2021. His research interests include Wireless Communication Networks, Blockchain and Artificial Intelligence targeting applications majorly towards industry 4.0/5.0. Read More

Dr. Sugeerth Murugesan

Sugeerth Murugesan is currently a Senior Data Scientist at Walgreens, San Francisco, USA. Previously, he served as a Machine Learning Engineer at Intel Corporation in the San Francisco Bay Area, USA. He also worked with Adobe Research and Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory as a researcher. He earned his Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) from the Computer Science Department at the University of California, Davis, where he was mentored by Bernd Hamann, Gunther Weber, and Kristofer Bouchard. Throughout his academic journey, Sugeerth has made significant contributions to the field, with multiple publications in renowned bioinformatics and computer science venues such as Computer Graphics Applications, BMC, Brain KDD in ACM-BCB, and Computational Biology (IEEE TCBB). His outstanding research achievements were acknowledged with a prestigious Best Paper Award at ACM-BCB. Sugeerth's research interests revolve around Network Science, Unsupervised Clustering, and Data Visualization, reflecting his dedication to advancing these domains. Read More
